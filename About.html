ABOUT US

Who Are We?

A premium appliances company established in 1981 with a history spanning more than 2 decades, today the JACKPOT Manufacturing Company is at the very forefront of product innovation in PAKISTAN. Through honesty, excellent services and strongly integrated business ethics we have been able to earn our reputation for quality, ingenuity, and value. Jackpot competes neck to neck with top brands in Pakistan in most major home appliance categories including air coolers, washing machines, food processors and other kitchen appliances such as blenders, mincers, coffee makers etc.

Business creation by bringing technological innovation to market

As pioneers of home and kitchen appliances, JACKPOT always stays ahead in the competitive market by showing dedicated focus on the technology and skills required in the manufacturing of our wide range of products qualifying our marketing and distribution to be carried out NATIONWIDE and striving to make your experience simple to achieve customer satisfaction. We turn ideas into opportunity.

Our Designs

JACKPOT appliances have simple designs with classic forms giving them a timeless look which reinforces their superior durability and quality so that we can help people turn their houses into homes. Every appliance is designed to help you manage your home exactly the way you want — easily and quickly.

Customer Care and Support

We offer a product warranty validation period when Jackpot Products have been purchased and under normal use to facilitate our valued customers. We also have our service centres with expert technicians set up in all parts of the country in case of any problem arising after purchase at our customers’ end.
